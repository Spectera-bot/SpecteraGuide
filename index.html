# The Story

Spectera, the name you know the bot by today, was not the initial name. On April 3 2021 I made this bot with the initial name projectHITLER which was also not the name, I was planning to name it to HITLER when the bot would be released. And as a lazy person at the starting on the project i used DiscordPYopen in new window on the thought i would write less code, but with lack of python skills i stepped back after a month and moved to my native language JavaScript and rewrited the bot to DiscordJSopen in new window which was a bit fun to do but as a result of lack of patience and stuff to maintain the bot, i stopped it and after two months i came back, Rewritten it with the name Obligatoropen in new window with MongoDBopen in new window as the initial database with lots of commands and features. But then i learnt TypeScriptopen in new window and wanted to make Obligator better so i made a custom handler in 1 week and then moved to Sapphireopen in new window which was a flexible framework after i knew discord.js was taking Sapphire into it after Commando's deprecation then renamed it to Spectera after a while, now Spectera uses PostgreSQL with Prisma and Sapphire with Typescript
